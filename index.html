FAQ

Before you ask

Does Cortex AI custody my funds?+

No. Cortex connects to your own wallet and only submits transactions you've pre-approved through your configured guardrails. Keys never leave your machine.

Which chains and markets are supported?+

Polymarket arbitrage scanning plus airdrop tracking across six major EVM and non-EVM chains, with new integrations added regularly.

Do I need to run a server?+

No. Cortex runs as a local application. An optional always-on mode is available for users who want continuous coverage on their own infrastructure.

Is there a cost to use it?+

Cortex AI is currently free to download during the early access period. Pricing details for future tiers will be communicated in-app.
